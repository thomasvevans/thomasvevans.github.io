_Fresh penne pasta with that perfect bite — made with just flour, eggs, and a bit of love. Once you try it, you’ll never go back._

---

## 📝 Ingredients

- 400g (3¼ cups) semolina flour or 00 flour  
- 4 large eggs  
- Pinch of salt  
- *Optional:* a splash of olive oil  

---

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions

### Step 1: Make the Dough  
1. On a clean surface or in a large bowl, mound the flour and create a well.  
2. Crack the eggs into the well, add a pinch of salt.  
3. Gradually incorporate flour into the eggs with a fork, then knead into a dough.  
4. Knead for 10 minutes until smooth and elastic. Wrap and let rest 30 minutes.

### Step 2: Shape the Penne  
1. Roll the dough out into sheets (2–3 mm thick).  
2. Cut into small rectangles, roll each around a wooden dowel or pasta tool to shape into penne tubes.  
3. Let dry on a floured surface or pasta rack.

*Alternatively:* Use a pasta extruder with a penne die for quicker, uniform results.

---

## 🫒 Serving Suggestion

Toss with your favourite sauce — **arrabbiata**, **pesto**, or **carbonara**. Pairs well with a robust **Montepulciano d'Abruzzo**.

---

## ✨ Chef's Tip

Use semolina for a firmer bite and better sauce grip. Let pasta dry for 15–20 minutes before cooking to help it hold shape.

Welcome to **Garlic Soffritto** — a cozy corner of the internet devoted to the beauty, soul, and simplicity of **Italian cooking**.

Founded by a lifelong lover of cucina casalinga (home cooking), Garlic Soffritto is more than just a recipe site — it's a celebration of seasonal ingredients, family traditions, and the honest flavors that define Italy’s regional cuisines.

Whether you’re simmering a Sunday ragù, learning to fold delicate agnolotti, or figuring out which olive oil really matters, you’re in the right place.

- 🥘 **Tried-and-true Italian recipes**, from lasagne alla Bolognese to wild herb risottos

- 🛒 **Product reviews** for pantry staples, imported goods, and tools worth your time

## Why "Garlic Soffritto"?

The **soffritto** is the aromatic base of countless Italian dishes — gently cooked onions, garlic, celery, and carrot.  
It’s humble, essential, and foundational — just like great food should be.

And garlic? Well, it speaks for itself.
